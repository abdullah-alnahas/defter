This page exists to exercise the hidden left section navigator. Hover the small strip on the left edge to reveal a list of every `<h2>` and `<h3>` on the page; clicking jumps to that section.

## Why a separate page

The earlier samples were short on purpose. To see the section navigator and the spacing rhythm at scale, a page needs to actually have sections.

### Spacing rhythm

A section navigator only earns its keep on pages where the reader might want to skip ahead. On short pages it's noise.

## On structure

Document structure is a contract with the reader. Headings promise: *here is a new idea*. The navigator turns that promise into navigation.

### Three things

1. Headings should be honest.
2. They should be parallel.
3. They should be few.
